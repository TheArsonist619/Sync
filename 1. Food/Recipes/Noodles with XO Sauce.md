## Ingredients

Serves 2 to 4

- [ ] 1 pound (450 g) fresh egg noodles or other fresh wheat noodles
- [ ] 1 tablespoon (15 ml) light soy sauce or shoyu
- [ ] A dash of black or Chinkiang vinegar
- [ ] 2 to 4 tablespoons [[XO Sauce]], plus more to taste
- [ ] Chopped scallions, for topping
- [ ] Mung bean sprouts or shredded cabbage (optional)

## Directions

1. [ ] Cook noodles according to package directions. As soon as they're done cooking, place each portion in an individual serving bowl.

2. [ ] Top each bowl with a tablespoon of light soy sauce or shoyu and a dash of black or Chinkiang vinegar.

3. [ ] Add 2 to 4 tablespoons of [[XO Sauce]] to each bowl. Add some chopped scallions and toss with chopsticks to combine.

4. [ ] If desired, add a handful of mung bean sprouts or shredded cabbage to the water as you boil the noodles and serve them in the same bowl.

5. [ ] Serve immediately. Add more [[XO Sauce]] to taste.

## Notes

**Quick Assembly:**
This dish comes together in about 10-15 minutes and is perfect for a quick lunch or dinner. Most of the time is spent cooking the noodles.

**Customization:**
Adjust the amount of XO Pepperoni Sauce to your taste preference — start with 2 tablespoons and add more until you reach your desired richness and spice level.

**Active Time:** 10 minutes
**Total Time:** 15 minutes

## Special Equipment

Large pot, individual serving bowls, chopsticks
