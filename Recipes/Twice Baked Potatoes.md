## Ingredients
- [ ] 4 large baking potatoes
    
- [ ] 8 strips bacon
    
- [ ] 1 cup sour cream
    
- [ ] 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
    
- [ ] 8 green onions, sliced, divided
    
- [ ] ½ cup milk
    
- [ ] 4 tablespoons butter 
    
- [ ] ½ teaspoon salt
    
- [ ] ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
## Preparation

1. [ ] Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    
    ![an overhead view of everything to make the ultimate twice baked potatoes.](https://www.allrecipes.com/thmb/SbBHE6IvUMy9Jn4i4YDGZoo5M-Q=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale\(\):max_bytes\(150000\):strip_icc\(\):format\(webp\)/24332-ultimate-twice-baked-potato-DDMFS-step-01-1598-78266aba7f4949a88c8f0f45fa58bebf.jpg)
    
    Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
    
2. [ ] Bake potatoes in the preheated oven until tender, about 1 hour. Set potatoes aside until cool enough to handle.
    
    ![potatoes baked on a baking sheet.](https://www.allrecipes.com/thmb/EH-luPZzuB9JblR5t-UyiEFwZGI=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale\(\):max_bytes\(150000\):strip_icc\(\):format\(webp\)/24332-ultimate-twice-baked-potato-DDMFS-step-02-1599-45e63000878b4f408cdc5f10fb712410.jpg)
    
    Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
    
3. [ ] Meanwhile, cook bacon in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble, and set aside.
    
    ![bacon cooked until crispy in a skillet.](https://www.allrecipes.com/thmb/YeNm9Rqo3PpqcttXdnIsjlzjaWo=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale\(\):max_bytes\(150000\):strip_icc\(\):format\(webp\)/24332-ultimate-twice-baked-potato-DDMFS-step-03-1600-7b23012e72064e6297628d204f703a49.jpg)
    
    Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
    
4. [ ] Slice potatoes in half lengthwise; scoop flesh into a large bowl. Reserve skins.
    
    ![baked potatoes, halved, and scooped.](https://www.allrecipes.com/thmb/QEH59B-O-StZm5y58tJUaGi_UUY=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale\(\):max_bytes\(150000\):strip_icc\(\):format\(webp\)/24332-ultimate-twice-baked-potato-DDMFS-step-04-1603-24ea1395b2804e2da24fa1dd805c44df.jpg)
    
    Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
    
5. [ ] Add sour cream, ½ cup cheese, ½ green onions, milk, butter, salt, and black pepper to potato flesh; mix with a hand mixer until well blended and creamy.
    
    ![potato and seasonings mixed until creamy.](https://www.allrecipes.com/thmb/bcHZgzEdHr1TrP8aKQjtuuxEW3k=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale\(\):max_bytes\(150000\):strip_icc\(\):format\(webp\)/24332-ultimate-twice-baked-potato-DDMFS-step-05-11605-7ae53c17df3c458ebee610ca23e669f0.jpg)
    
    Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
    
6. [ ] Spoon potato mixture into reserved potato skins; top with remaining ½ cup cheese, remaining ½ green onions, and bacon.
    
    ![potato halves filled with seasoned mixture and topped with more cheddar cheese and crispy bacon.](https://www.allrecipes.com/thmb/N4Qqh6Q6LyfhS6Q-XzGaUGZLqOg=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale\(\):max_bytes\(150000\):strip_icc\(\):format\(webp\)/24332-ultimate-twice-baked-potato-DDMFS-step-06-1608-e8f1104a4f334030b43e321f79f5751f.jpg)
