## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    
- 1 small onion, chopped 
    
- 1 teaspoon salt
    
- 1 (1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger, chopped
    
- 2 plum tomatoes, cut into 1/2-inch dice
    
- 1 pound bone-in pork chops
    
- 4 cups water, more if needed
    
- 1 (1.41 ounce) package tamarind soup base (such as Knorr®)
    
- ½ pound fresh green beans, trimmed

## Preparation

1. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion; cook and stir until softened and translucent, about 5 minutes. Season with salt.
    
    ![Overhead of onions cooking on a skillet.](https://www.allrecipes.com/thmb/DWE3_ls3D1OcQJ_J-_fiisASH8E=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale\(\):max_bytes\(150000\):strip_icc\(\):format\(webp\)/204958-pork-sinigang-mfs-step-1-6a49a6c5f05941bbbbe91ed06a471532.jpg)
    
    Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
    
2. Stir in ginger, tomatoes, and pork chops. Cover and reduce heat to medium-low. Turn the pork occasionally, until browned.
    
    ![Overhead of two cuts of pork cooking on top of chopped vegetables in a skillet.](https://www.allrecipes.com/thmb/kCmAj90DeSiQcLMccO-OBr5c6DQ=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale\(\):max_bytes\(150000\):strip_icc\(\):format\(webp\)/204958-pork-sinigang-mfs-step-2-92d055ad6bf2419fa591ab7057a3feb4.jpg)
    
    Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
    
3. Pour in water and tamarind soup base. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the pork is tender and cooked through, about 30 minutes.
    
    ![Overhead of water and tamarind soup base being stirred and cooked with cuts of pork.](https://www.allrecipes.com/thmb/6d8x7C39lPBXN7HVzFInsIyh3AE=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale\(\):max_bytes\(150000\):strip_icc\(\):format\(webp\)/204958-pork-sinigang-mfs-step-3-3b3cc82cc5c24631b82c51e51003c375.jpg)
    
    Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
    
4. Stir in green beans and cook until tender.
