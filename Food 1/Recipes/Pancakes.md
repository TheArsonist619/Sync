## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1/4 sugar
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 2 1/4 milk
- 1/4 oil
- 1 tbsp vanilla
- 1/4 apple cider vinegar
- 3 eggs

## Preparation

- whisk dry stuff. whisky wet stuff. Combine and fold together 20-25 times. **Do not over mix.  Lumps are okay**
- salted whipped butter is preffered
- 1/4 cup per pancake. Do no mix when scooping. Do not press down
