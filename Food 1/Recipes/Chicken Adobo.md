## Ingredients

- 3 lbs chicken thigh
- 3/4 cup white vinegar
- 1/4 soy sauce
- 1/4 tsp peppercorns(crushed)
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 3 garlic cloves(chopped or crushed)
- 2 bay leaves
- 1/4 onion, chopped



## Preparation
- Marinate at least 1 hour
- Bring to boil and simmer for 40 minutes
