## Ingredients

- [ ] 7 cups water 
- [ ] 1 cup long-grain white rice 
- [ ] Two 4-inch cinnamon stick  
- [ ] One 14-ounce can condensed milk 
- [ ] 1 cup whole milk 
- [ ] Ground cinnamon, for dusting
## Directions

1. [ ] Put the water, rice, and 1 cinnamon stick in a medium-size heavy saucepan set over medium-high heat. 
2. [ ] Bring to a boil, uncovered, and cook until the rice is tender, about 18 minutes. Strain out the liquid, discard the cinnamon and reserve the rice. Return the rice to the saucepan. Stir in the evaporated milk, condensed milk, whole milk and cinnamon stick. Continue cooking over medium-high heat until the mixture comes to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and cook, uncovered, stirring constantly, until the mixture is thick, about 20 minutes.
3. [ ] Transfer the pudding to a serving bowl. Dust the top of the pudding with ground cinnamon and serve.
