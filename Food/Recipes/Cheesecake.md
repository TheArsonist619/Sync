## Ingredients

servings: 12 slices

**Graham Cracker Crust:**
- [ ] 1½ cups graham cracker crumbs (170g)
- [ ] 2 tablespoons sugar
- [ ] 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- [ ] 7 tablespoons melted butter

**Cheesecake:**
- [ ] 32 oz cream cheese, softened (910g)
- [ ] 1 cup sugar (200g)
- [ ] ⅔ cup sour cream (160g)
- [ ] 1½ teaspoons vanilla extract
- [ ] ⅛ teaspoon salt
- [ ] 4 large eggs, room temperature, lightly beaten

**Special Equipment:** 9-inch springform pan, foil, cookie sheet

## Directions

1. [ ] Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).

2. [ ] Combine graham cracker crumbs, sugars, and melted butter; press into springform pan.

3. [ ] Beat cream cheese until smooth; add sugar and mix.

4. [ ] Stir in sour cream, vanilla, and salt.

5. [ ] Gradually add beaten eggs on low speed until just combined.

6. [ ] Pour into prepared pan on foil-lined cookie sheet.

7. [ ] Bake 50-60 minutes at 325°F until edges puff slightly and center jiggles.

8. [ ] Cool 10 minutes; loosen crust from pan sides with knife.

9. [ ] Cool 1-2 hours at room temperature before refrigerating overnight.

## Notes

**Timing:**
- Prep Time: 20 minutes
- Bake Time: 1 hour 15 minutes
- Cooling Time: 6 hours
- Total Time: 7 hours 35 minutes

**Key Tips:**
- Use room-temperature ingredients for best results
- Avoid overmixing eggs to prevent cracks
- No water bath required

Cheesecake should be refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 5 days.
