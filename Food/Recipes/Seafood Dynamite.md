## Ingredients
servings:

- [ ] 4 cups mayo
- [ ] 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- [ ] ½ lemon
- [ ] 12 pieces scallops
- [ ] 4 pieces shrimp
- [ ] 4 pieces crab meat
- [ ] 6 large mushrooms

## Directions

1. [ ] Mix the mayo, soy sauce, and lemon juice in a bowl.
2. [ ] Partly boil the seafood to almost done.
3. [ ] Place all in a casserole.
4. [ ] Glaze with the sauce until the parts are covered.
5. [ ] Broil 3–4 minutes until golden brown.
